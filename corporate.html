College to Corporate Transition

Our Aim

This club is a platform to quench the thirst of job hunters.

Our Objectives

The role of the club is to give an insight about various aspects of the job interview right from the time you get a campus drive or interview call to interview preparation and finally selection and offer letter. The objectives of this club are listed below.

  1. To provide Career Guidance.
  2. To identify right Job Opportunities.
  3. To prepare for a job interview with frequently asked Technical & HR Interview Questions and answers.
  4. To provide interview tips, techniques and preparatory materials to help our students to participate in interview like face to face, online, telephonic or Walk-in interview on campus and Off campus drive.

Activities

  1. Group Discussion
  2. Mock Interview
  3. Resume Writing
  4. Telephonic Interview
