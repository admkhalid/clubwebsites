E - CLUB
The Legion of Entrepreneurs

Our Aim

The aim of the club is to further the understanding about new and small businesses and promote campus startups. 

Our Objectives

  1. Identify the entrepreneurship potential among students.
  2. Create platform for cross functional skill exhibition.
  3. To teach various methods of solving programming problems in different points of view.
  4. Opportunities for exhibiting their entrepreneurial skills at college campus
  5. To provide mentorship for students

Activities

  • Conduct weekly activities to promote start ups
  • Connect to mentors in all areas of discipline.
  • Startup idea contest
  • Entrepreneur speaker series
  • Entrepreneurs Success story - Movie & Discussion

Who can join?

Students from all departments and years can join the club.

Members will be notified of any upcoming events and activities.
