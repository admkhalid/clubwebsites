JOHN KEATS LITERARY CLUB

Our Aim

The aim of the club is to enhance and enrich English as a language.

Our Objectives

  1. To improve reading and writing skills.
  2. To inculcate Spoken English.
  3. To enhance effective thinking and creative abilities of the students.
